Client Report

  • Client

    As a client I think things went ok. I gave my lead dev everything they needed. My wireframe was ugly but they did a decent job with it. We communicated over email and it worked fine. In the end I think the site wasn't very pretty but that could be my fault because of the color schemes I gave him. If I had to do this again I would use an actual program for my wireframe and choose better colors.

  • Lead

    Communicating with my client was easy. She sent a very detailed wireframe so I didn't have to make any decisions on my own. I emailed by junior devs and let them know what their roles are but they never did anything on their pages. Publishing the site with GitHub was easy.

  • Junior 1-Winters

    Working on the site for Winters was really easy. The lead dev got his header and footer done quickly and I got my page done quickly as well. There was one time they force pushed a change that messed up my stuff but GitHub is a magical place so it didn't cause any problems in the long run.
